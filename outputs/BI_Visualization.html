Closing / Recommendation

Own the corridor and the feeder
in the same plan, or lose both.

We placed 19 stations against a 614,412 BEV forecast and found 35 zones where firm capacity cannot serve 2027 demand. Fourteen sit on i-DE feeders. One programme converts a compliance cost into an 18.6 MW network asset.

Ask 01
Approve 15 priority grid access requests
R1 access on A-6, A-3, A-4 nodes. Lock transformer slots Q3 2026.
Ask 02
Co-site charging with reinforcement
Pair 3 Congested sites with reinforcement works. Cut commissioning by 6 months.
Ask 03
Publish a 2027 corridor scorecard
Adopt Strategic KPI tier as public reporting, anchored on PNIEC 2030.
