Closing / Recommendation

Own the corridor and the feeder
in the same plan, or lose both.

We placed 19 stations against a 614,412 BEV forecast and found 35 zones where firm capacity cannot serve 2027 demand. Fourteen sit on i-DE feeders. One programme converts a compliance cost into an 18.6 MW network asset.
